Talia's Story

Talia Smith was a healthy professional and caregiver to her disabled veteran husband until taking Ciprofloxacin in April 2021. After just 3 pills, she was hospitalized and developed severe complications including Mast Cell Activation Syndrome, leaving her wheelchair-bound and requiring 24/7 care. Your donation helps Talia access vital alternative treatments not covered by insurance as she continues fighting for her life.
